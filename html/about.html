About E-Tabi

E-Tabi is a storage-tracking application that is used to keep track of a storage unit renter’s items that they place inside or remove from their unit and contains several useful features that help with the overall management process. From seeing the images and general descriptions of the items they have, to keeping track of the days left before rent is due and having a complete history of when the unit is accessed to what items are placed inside or removed, to even a system where they could generate an alternative, and having a digital key to access their storage unit, E-Tabi has many convenient features as long as they are utilized to their full potential.


Mission

We aspire to help users keep track of their various belongings of differing value, whether they be of sentimental or practical value, and make the process of acquiring and storing items be more convenient to all. Overall, people may have things that aren’t easy to part with and the mere idea of them being stored safely ease their minds, this application was made to add another level of ease towards that way of thinking through its many features.

Packages

For our packages, we used the following: Express, Body-Parser, Mongoose, QrCode, Path, Multer.
